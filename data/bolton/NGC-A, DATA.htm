NOTE

 

All New General Catalogue files begin NGC.

Then:

NGC-CATSUP = names of taxa in the family-group (suprageneric taxa), in alphabetical order.

NGC-GEN.A-L and NGC-GEN.M-Z = names of taxa in the genus-group in alphabetical order.

NGC-REFS. = references (full bibliography), in alphabetical order by author.

NGC-Sp = names in the species-group, listed in alphabetical order by genus-group names, for example:

NGC-Spa-an = names in the species-group from the genus-group name Acalama to the genus-group name Antillaemyrmex, in alphabetical order.

NGC-Sptet-z = names in the species-group from the genus-group name Tetraponera to the genus-group name Zherichinius, in alphabetical order.
